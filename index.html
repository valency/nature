Biography

I received my degree of PhD in Computer Science and Engineering in 2016 supervised by Prof. Qian Zhang from the Department of Computer Science and Engineering of The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology.

I received my degree of MPhil in Computer Science and Technology in 2012 from the HKUST Fok Ying Tung Graduate School of The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology.

I received my degree of BSc in Software Technology and Application in 2010 from the Faculty of Information Technology of Macau University of Science and Technology.

Research Interest

I am interested in data mining and bioinformatics.
